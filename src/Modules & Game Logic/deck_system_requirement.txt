Deck: where you draw from, has the cards
	Includes all three cards at minimum (fireball, shield, heal)
	Could include duplicates
Hand: what the player uses 
	Can only include as many cards as deck has
	if deck has three fireball, three heal, three shield, hand cannot have more than three fireball, more than three shield, nor more than three heal
	Can have duplicates
Discard pile: where used cards go
	If card gets used from hand, it goes to discard pile
	reset card deck
	No impact on hand
	Discard pile becomes empty
	Deck will get restored, cards return to it
shuffle card deck
	Changes order of contents in deck
	No impact on hand
	No impact on discard pile	
	No creation/removal of cards
